How It Began

Born in a Nairobi Kitchen, 2018

Brewed Awakenings started when founder Amara Njoki returned from a barista training trip to Melbourne and couldn't find the quality she'd experienced anywhere in Nairobi. She started roasting single-origin Kenyan and Ethiopian beans in her home kitchen, sharing them with neighbours.

Word spread quickly. By 2019, she had opened our first small counter in Westlands. By 2021, we were named one of Nairobi's top specialty coffee spots. Today, we employ 18 people and source from 6 farms across East Africa.

Traceability

Every bag of beans can be traced back to the specific farm and harvest season. No mystery, no middlemen.

🤝

Fair Partnerships

We pay a minimum 30% above fair-trade prices to every farmer we work with — because good coffee requires good livelihoods.

🌱

Sustainability

Compostable cups, biodegradable packaging, and a used-grounds program for local urban farmers.

🎓

Community Training

We run free barista workshops monthly, open to anyone in Westlands who wants to learn the craft.
